# Privacy Policy for TBreak App

**Last Updated: December 26, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how TBreak App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** If you create an account, we may collect your email address and username.
- **Usage Data:** Information about your break tracking sessions and progress.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** We may collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Statistics:** We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.

### Information from Third Parties

**Google Play Billing:**
- We use Google Play Billing to process purchases and donations
- Google handles all payment processing
- We do not store credit card information
- Purchase history is managed by Google Play

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App
- Process your purchases and donations through Google Play
- Track your break sessions (stored locally on your device)
- Improve and optimize the App
- Send you important updates about the App
- Respond to your support requests

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** Most of your data (break tracking, preferences) is stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Sync:** If enabled, some data may be synced across your devices
- **Security:** We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
- **No Sale of Data:** We do not sell your personal information to third parties

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Google Play Billing
- **Purpose:** Process in-app purchases and donations
- **Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

- Purchases are subject to Google Play's terms and refund policy
